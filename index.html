Omelette Recipe

Prep

5 mins

Cook

5 mins

Total

10 mins

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
    room temperature
  • 1/4 cup whole milk
  • 1/4 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter

Instructions

Beat the eggs and milk until light and frothy.

Heat a nonstick skillet over medium head. Add the butter.

Pour in the eggs. Let them set for a few seconds.
